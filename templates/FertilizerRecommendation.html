    Fertilizer Recommendation Tool

    The Fertilizer Recommendation Tool is designed to assist you in selecting the most suitable fertilizer for your crop based on its nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium (NPK) requirements. Simply select your crop from the dropdown list and enter the NPK ratio suitable for your crop.

    The tool will analyze this information and provide you with a recommendation for the best fertilizer to use. This recommendation will help you ensure that your crops receive the nutrients they need for healthy growth and optimal yields.

    Using the Fertilizer Recommendation Tool can help you make informed decisions about fertilization, leading to more efficient use of resources and better crop performance. Whether you're a small-scale farmer or a large agricultural enterprise, this tool can help you achieve the best results from your fertilization practices.
