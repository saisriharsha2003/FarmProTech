    Crop Recommendation Tool

    The Crop Suggestion Tool is designed to help you determine the most suitable crop to grow on your farm. By providing information such as the NPK ratio, humidity percentage, rainfall, pH level, and temperature of your farm, the tool can suggest crops that are best suited to your conditions.

    Once you enter these details, the tool will analyze them and provide you with a crop recommendation. This recommendation will include information about the suggested crop, such as its ideal growing conditions and potential yields. Armed with this information, you can make informed decisions about what to plant on your farm to maximize your yield and success.

    Using the Crop Suggestion Tool can help you optimize your farming practices and improve your overall farm productivity. Whether you're a seasoned farmer looking to diversify your crops or a novice looking for guidance, this tool can provide you with valuable insights to support your farming endeavors.
